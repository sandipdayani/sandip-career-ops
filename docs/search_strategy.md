## Filters

- Location: USA, Canada, remote USA/Canada, relocation supported.
- Seniority: PhD, research scientist, senior engineer, staff engineer, postdoc, research associate.
- Exclude when needed: technician, maintenance, sales, CAD drafter, production supervisor, entry level.
