Title: Computer Modeling & Simulation Specialist / Engineer
Company: General Dynamics Land Systems-Canada
Location: London, Ontario, Canada
Employment type: 1-year fixed-term contract
Source: https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/view/4406861529/

The role supports computer modeling and simulation for GDLS-C vehicle and
subsystem design teams and internal R&D personnel. Responsibilities include
running and interpreting simulations for military vehicles, subsystems,
competitor vehicles, and vendor products; supporting optimum design and
requirements compliance; and improving modeling/simulation methods and
procedures.

Key work includes advanced simulations of military vehicles and subsystems,
structural analysis, material fatigue simulations, simulation support for
product development, technical reporting, R&D validation testing support,
test-plan/report preparation, data reduction and analysis, technical support to
design engineers, and new product development simulations.

Required background includes a university degree or college diploma, at least
3 years relevant experience, 1 to 3 years in CAE preferably in automotive,
defense, or aerospace, FEA software knowledge, automotive subsystem knowledge,
stress analysis, mechanics, dynamics, mathematics, communication skills, and
ability to travel.

Preferred qualifications include P.Eng or eligibility, advanced university
degree, NX NASTRAN, Altair FEA tools, heavy off-road vehicle engineering,
defense design standards, and Glyphworks.
